Services

Ambulatory services

At RescueRx, we prioritize speed and care when it comes to emergencies. Our ambulatory service ensures that a fully equipped ambulance, with a team of trained paramedics, reaches you within 10 minutes of your call. Our fleet includes Advanced Life Support (ALS) and Basic Life Support (BLS) vehicles, each designed to handle a variety of medical emergencies, from critical injuries to minor health concerns. Whether you’re at work, home, or an industrial site, our goal is to deliver lifesaving care right when you need it.

E-Pharmacy

Get your prescription and over-the-counter medications delivered directly to your home with RescueRx E-Pharmacy. We offer a simple, fast, and reliable way to order your medicines online. From daily health supplements to critical prescriptions, our e-pharmacy ensures that you have access to the medications you need, exactly when you need them. Place your orders easily through our platform and rest assured that your medications will arrive on time, every time.

Lab tests

At RescueRx, we make health check-ups and diagnostics easy and accessible. Our lab testing services cover a wide range of tests, from routine health screenings to specialized diagnostics. With the convenience of home sample collection, you no longer need to visit a lab to get tested. We ensure accurate results, delivered quickly, and make it easy for you to monitor your health from the comfort of your home. Trust RescueRx for precise, hassle-free diagnostic services.

Consultation services

Need medical advice but not sure if you should visit a hospital? RescueRx Consultation Services connects you with experienced healthcare professionals who can provide personalized health consultations from the comfort of your home. Whether it’s a routine check-up, post-treatment consultation, or immediate medical advice, our team of doctors and specialists is available for telehealth consultations, guiding you toward the best course of action for your health.

Plans

Corporate

In the corporate world, employee health and safety are top priorities. We ensures that businesses can provide rapid medical assistance during workplace emergencies.
