About us

At RescueRx, we understand that in a medical emergency, every second can make a difference. Our mission is simple but critical: to provide fast, reliable, and efficient ambulatory services to those in need—within 10 minutes of an emergency call

We are more than just an ambulance service. With a team of highly trained medical professionals and state-of-the-art vehicles, RescueRx is equipped to handle emergencies of all scales, offering a lifeline when it matters most. Whether it’s a critical care situation, an accident, or an urgent health issue, we are committed to delivering top-tier medical assistance right to your doorstep in record time.
